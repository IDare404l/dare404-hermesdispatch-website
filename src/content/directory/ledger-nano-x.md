## Overview

The Ledger Nano X is a hardware wallet that stores cryptocurrency private keys offline. It supports thousands of coins and connects via USB or Bluetooth to the Ledger Live mobile app.

## What Makes It Interesting

- Offline key storage protects against malware and phishing
- Bluetooth support for mobile use
- Supports BTC, ETH, and most major altcoins

## Limitations

- Premium price vs. software wallets
- Requires physical device and recovery seed backup
- Past data-breach incidents affected marketing data, not private keys

## General Notes

**Good fit for:** Anyone holding more crypto than they can afford to lose on an exchange.
**Less ideal for:** Active traders making frequent transactions.

## FAQ

**Is a hardware wallet necessary?**
For significant holdings, yes. Exchanges can be hacked or freeze withdrawals.

**Ledger Nano X vs Nano S Plus?**
Nano X has Bluetooth and more storage for apps. Nano S Plus is cheaper and USB-only.

**What if I lose the device?**
Your recovery seed (24 words) restores access. Never store it digitally or share it.
