## Overview

The Framework Desktop brings the company's modular, repairable philosophy to a mini-ITX tower. It is designed to take standard desktop GPUs without proprietary locks.

## What Makes It Interesting

- Standard GPU slot; not soldered or external-box limited
- Modular mainboard, power supply, and I/O
- Compact footprint for a desktop GPU workstation

## Limitations

- You still need to buy and install the GPU separately
- Thermal headroom depends on chosen GPU and case config
- Smaller ecosystem than established SFF builders

## General Notes

**Good fit for:** Tinkerers, repairability advocates, long-term upgraders.
**Less ideal for:** Plug-and-play buyers or those wanting maximum GPU thermals.
