The most trusted mining profitability calculator. Plug in your hardware and power cost, get exact daily/weekly/monthly revenue estimates.

Supports:
- **ASIC miners** — Antminer, Whatsminer, Avalon

- **Power cost input** — Enter $/kWh for your area (Boise: ~$0.09/kWh)
