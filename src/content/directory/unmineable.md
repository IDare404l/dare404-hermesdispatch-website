## Overview

UnMineable is a mining pool aimed at beginners who want to mine with whatever hardware they already own and receive payouts in their preferred coin.

## What Makes It Interesting

- Auto-converts mined coins to your payout choice
- Works with CPU and GPU rigs
- No exchange account required

## Limitations

- Pool fees eat into thin margins
- Not optimized for industrial ASIC farms
- Payout thresholds vary by coin

## General Notes

**Good fit for:** Hobby miners with existing PCs.
**Less ideal for:** Large-scale operations with direct pool relationships.
