## Overview

The Trezor Safe 3 is a beginner-friendly hardware wallet with open-source firmware. It is the best alternative for users who prefer transparency over Ledger's secure-element approach.

## When to choose

- You prefer open-source firmware
- You want a simple, secure hardware wallet
- You hold Bitcoin, Ethereum, and major altcoins
